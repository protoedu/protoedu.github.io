Our Work

MediaPond Is an augmented reality projector and social display developed with Sony Computer Science Lab(CSL in Tokyo). The experience includes place based media sharing with users that interact with MediaPond. Users are able to share messages and media with other users through the 'pond'. The circular display is supported by it's UI which includes native polar coordinate interactions. Users have the experience of interacting in a fun 'Lazy Susan' circular digital augmented world. This work was carried out as fundamental research with the Human Computer Interface Group.
